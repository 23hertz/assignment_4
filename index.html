Project overview

During the Covid-19 era, Netflix launches an advertising campaign spotlighting their out of work crew member. On May 10-13 of 2020, advertisements run in different locations around the county including the 17 billboards and more than 100 boards shelters. These adds contains faces and names of selected 17 crew members, along with hopefull message: "You will work in this town again."
